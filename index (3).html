Phase 03 — EDL

SEVEN
MINUTES
OF TERROR

Entry, Descent and Landing. The spacecraft hits the atmosphere at 20,000 km/h. From the moment it touches air to the moment it touches ground — 7 minutes. Mission Control watches. Waits. Prays.

🔥
ATMOSPHERIC ENTRY
Heat shield sustains 1,650°C. Ionized plasma blacks out all communications for 7 minutes. Mission Control hears nothing.
🪂
SUPERSONIC CHUTES
21-metre Technora parachutes deploy at Mach 1.7. The vehicle decelerates from 500 m/s to 80 m/s in 90 seconds.
🚀
POWERED DESCENT
Retro-rockets fire at 1.4km altitude. The radar altimeter maps the ground 1,200 times per second, picking the flattest spot.
🌑
TOUCHDOWN
A gentle thud. Dust settles. The crew is motionless. Then someone whispers: "We're on Mars." Then everyone cries.
